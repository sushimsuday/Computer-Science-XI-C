In Assamese, the script and phonetic representations do not align perfectly with the A-Z structure of the Latin alphabet. However, here is an attempt to map the Assamese letters to their closest phonetic counterparts in the A-Z range:


### Assamese Letters Corresponding to A-Z Phonetics

2. **B** - ব (ba)
3. **C** - চ (ca)
4. **D** - দ (da), ড (ḍa)
5. **E** - এ (e)

18. **R** - ৰ (ra)
19. **S** - স (sa), শ (sha)
20. **T** - ত (ta), ট (ṭa)

Note: The Assamese script doesn't have direct equivalents for every English letter, so the mapping is based on the closest phonetic sounds.
